Equipment & Facilities

Our laboratory is equipped with state-of-the-art laser systems and material characterization tools to support cutting-edge research.

Laboratory Equipment

High-Power Fiber Laser System

2021
Cutting Welding Surface Treatment

Technical Specifications:

  • Power: 2kW continuous wave
  • Wavelength: 1070nm
  • Beam Quality: M² < 1.1
  • Positioning System: 3-axis CNC with 1000mm x 1000mm x 500mm working area
  • Control System: Integrated touchscreen interface with custom software

Our high-power fiber laser system enables precise cutting, welding, and surface treatment of a wide range of materials including metals, ceramics, and composites. The system includes an integrated chiller unit, beam delivery optics, and gas assist capabilities.

The system can be configured for different processing modes including cutting with nitrogen, oxygen, or compressed air, welding with or without filler material, and surface treatment with controlled atmosphere.

Service Charges (per hour):

Academic Rate: ₹2,500
Industry Rate: ₹5,000

Femtosecond Laser Micromachining Station

2022
Micromachining Ablation Surface Texturing

Technical Specifications:

  • Pulse Duration: < 400 fs
  • Wavelength: 1030nm (fundamental), 515nm and 343nm (harmonics)
  • Repetition Rate: Single pulse to 1 MHz
  • Max Pulse Energy: 400 μJ at 1030nm
  • Positioning System: 5-axis with nanometer resolution
  • Working Area: 200mm x 200mm x 100mm

The femtosecond laser micromachining station provides non-thermal material removal capabilities for precision structuring of virtually any material. The ultrafast laser pulses enable ablation without heat-affected zones, allowing for extremely precise material processing.

This system is ideal for creating microstructures, surface texturing, and precision micromachining of sensitive materials including polymers, biological tissues, ceramics, semiconductors, and metals.

Service Charges (per hour):

Academic Rate: ₹3,000
Industry Rate: ₹6,000

Advanced Materials Characterization Suite

2019
Material Analysis Surface Characterization Mechanical Testing

Technical Specifications:

  • Scanning Electron Microscope: Resolution down to 3nm with EDS capabilities
  • X-Ray Diffractometer: 2-theta range from 5° to 140°
  • Micro-hardness Tester: Load range from 10g to 1kg
  • 3D Surface Profilometer: Vertical resolution of 0.1nm
  • Optical Microscope: Magnification up to 1000x with digital imaging

Our comprehensive characterization suite enables detailed analysis of material microstructure, phase composition, surface topography, and mechanical properties. This integrated facility supports both qualitative and quantitative analysis of processed materials.

The suite is extensively used for analyzing laser-processed samples, evaluating surface modifications, and correlating processing parameters with material properties.

Service Charges (per hour):

Academic Rate: ₹2,000
Industry Rate: ₹4,500

Selective Laser Melting System

2020
Additive Manufacturing Rapid Prototyping

Technical Specifications:

  • Laser Power: 400W fiber laser
  • Build Volume: 250mm x 250mm x 325mm
  • Layer Thickness: 20-100μm
  • Scan Speed: Up to 7m/s
  • Materials: Stainless steel, aluminum alloys, titanium alloys, nickel alloys
  • Atmosphere Control: Inert gas (N₂ or Ar)

The selective laser melting system enables the creation of complex metal parts with intricate internal features that would be impossible to produce using conventional manufacturing methods. The system builds parts layer by layer from metal powder that is selectively melted by a high-power fiber laser.

Applications include rapid prototyping of functional metal components, manufacturing of optimized structures through topology optimization, and production of customized implants for biomedical applications.

Service Charges (per hour):

Academic Rate: ₹3,500
Industry Rate: ₹7,000

Note: Material costs are additional and charged based on actual usage.
